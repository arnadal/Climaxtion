What We Have Done

Look at how Abeer contributes to save Earth.


Go Solar:

The sun can have you sweat on a summer day. However, don't forget: it assists Earth too! Not only geograpically, but environmentally as well. The use of solar panels can help us minimize the use of fossil fuels being used to generate electricity.



Manufacture... But Appropriately:

Manufacturers should prefer using environmentally secure materials for packaging and for materials used in items. Plastic should not be used as a package, or a part of a toy.



Save Trees, Save Lives:

Cutting down forests means cutting down trees. Cutting them down can minimize rainfall due to reduction of water vapor. It also increases the level of Carbon Dioxide present in the air as the process of photosynthesis reduces.



Consume Wisely:

Your home and use of power matters. Not only because of the frustrating bills, but mainly due to environmental conditions. Yes, environmental conditions may influence your billing. Use of solar equipment can optimize consumption of electricity.
